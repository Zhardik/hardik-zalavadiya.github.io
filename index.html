About Me

Bridging Power Systems
& Artificial Intelligence

I am a Research Scientist at the DLR Institute of Networked Energy Systems in Oldenburg, Germany, where I develop AI-driven control strategies for smart grid voltage regulation, renewable energy integration, and distributed energy management.

My work sits at the intersection of power systems engineering and applied machine learning — designing reinforcement learning controllers, co-simulation frameworks connecting MATLAB/Simulink with Python-based algorithms, and real-time monitoring dashboards for grid operations.

With a background in M.Sc. Mechatronics from Universität Siegen and hands-on industry experience as an electrical engineer in India's power distribution sector, I bring a unique blend of theoretical depth and practical engineering to every project.
